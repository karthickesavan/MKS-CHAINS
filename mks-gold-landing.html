ABOUT MKS CHAINS

Four Decades of Craftsmanship.
One Legacy of Trust.

MKS Chains is a trusted wholesale manufacturer and supplier of premium Gold Thali Saradu chains. Built on decades of expertise, we proudly serve jewellery retailers with superior craftsmanship, consistent quality, and timeless traditional designs.

M. Kaja Moideen

Founder

With over 40 years of experience in the jewellery industry, M. Kaja Moideen laid the foundation of MKS Chains through dedication, precision, and uncompromising quality. His deep knowledge of traditional Gold Thali Saradu craftsmanship has earned the trust of jewellery retailers across Tamil Nadu and beyond.

K. Sheik Meeran

Proprietor

A Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) graduate, K. Sheik Meeran leads MKS Chains with a modern business approach while preserving its rich heritage. As Proprietor, he focuses on delivering reliable wholesale supply, strengthening retailer relationships, and expanding the company's vision for the future.
